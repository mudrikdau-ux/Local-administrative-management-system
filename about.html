About LAMS

Digital platform for modern local governance

What is LAMS?

The Local Administration Management System (LAMS) is a web-based platform that modernizes how local governments serve their communities. It centralizes citizen records, document processing, payments, and communication into one digital hub.

Our Mission

To provide efficient, transparent, and accessible digital administrative services to local communities.

Our Vision

To become a digitally empowered local administration delivering high-quality services to all citizens.
